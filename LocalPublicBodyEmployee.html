State Of New Mexico Group Benefits Plan
Getting Started as an LPB New Hire
  • Check with your Employer to Confirm your Eligiblity Window; in most cases you have 31 days from your Date of Hire to enroll in benefits.
  • If you are adding Dependents please make sure to send required documents. Click here for a list.
  • Your New Hire Benefits Instruction Sheet is Important! Click Here for a copy.
Getting Started as a Legislator
  • Legislators have 31 days from Date of Appointment to enroll in benefits.
  • If you are adding Dependents please make sure to send required documents. Click here for a list.
  • Click Here for the Special Legislator Enrollment Form.
  • Legislators will Receive Monthly Self-Pay statements from ASD by email. Contact 505-244-6000 for any billing questions.
  • Please note that Legislators are not eligible for disability and may not elect a POP waiver.
