    Application Process Troubleshooting

    How do I become eligible for disability benefits?

    • The State of New Mexico Disability Policy is a self-insured plan which was created to provide financial assistance to those that are unable to work for a period of time and lose income due to a sickness or injury (if not receiving Workers Compensation). This Disability Plan is not available to dependents. Participation in this Plan is voluntary. The premium is 100% paid by the employee after-tax.

    Application Process Troubleshooting

    Greetings SoNM and LPB Employees

    • Virta, the newly added benefit for employees covered under Presbyterian or BlueCross BlueShield plans, has brought to our attention some issues employees may be encountering when applying to the Virta program.

    • If you've attempted to create an account (whether from your State email or from an alternate email address), and are now locked out, please send an email to support@virtahealth.com. Virta will generate a help ticket and will contact you with further instructions to reset your account.

    • You may also have been blocked from the site by your department's firewall. If so, please reach out to your IT department and ask that Virta be whitelisted so that you may utilize this State-sponsored benefit.

      • For IT Department

    • Please add virtahealth.com and *.virtahealth.com (that is: virtahealth and all subdomains of virtahealth, which would include apply.virtahealth.com) to the Department's whitelist so that this State-sponsored benefit may be accessed.

    • If you have any further issues with the Virta program, please contact Virta directly at: support@virtahealth.com or visit

      For more information about Virta Heath visit virtahealth.com/join/sonm

    • Thank you for your patience as we continue to work to bring you tools that will help you live a healthier life.
